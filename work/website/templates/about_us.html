About Us

We're a team of passionate freshman engineers creating innovative IoT solutions for mountain climbers, focused on safety, health monitoring, and emergency response.

Our Mission

At MCHGT, our mission is to leverage IoT technology to create a safer environment for mountain climbers. We understand the challenges and dangers faced during high-altitude adventures, and we're committed to developing solutions that monitor health metrics in real-time.

We make sure your health & safety are prioritized on every climb

With MCHGT, climbers can focus on their journey while our devices quietly monitor their health status, automatically alerting them when rest is needed and providing crucial data to medical teams in emergency situations.
